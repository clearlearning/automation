How to use:

  1. In TALENTLMS, under the ADMINISTRATOR role, hover over the GO TO menu and select REPORTS.
  2. Select SCORM REPORTS from the menu on the right.
  3. Select the appropriate module from the list. Use the SEARCH bar at the bottom of the list as needed. Next, locate the appropriate user record and scroll to the right.
  4. Click on the MAGNIFYING GLASS icon that pops up when you hover over the OPTIONS column.
  5. At the bottom of the pop up window will be a section labeled SUSPEND DATA. Copy all contents in that section and paste above.
  6. Click the PARSE DATA button to view the progress report for the module and user selected.
